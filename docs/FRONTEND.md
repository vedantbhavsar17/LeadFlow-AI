## Future UX Principles

- Lead conversion workflow should be the primary navigation model.
- CRM screens should support conversion, not dominate the product.
- Business Context should be easy for non-technical owners to maintain.
- AI outputs should be previewable, editable, and explainable.
- WhatsApp and LinkedIn MVP workflows should focus on generated copy and copy-to-clipboard.

Phase 1 does not implement pages or components.
