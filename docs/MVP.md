# MVP Scope

## Required

- NVIDIA NIM as primary AI provider.
- Free-service-friendly AI architecture.
- SQLite as primary MVP database.
- PostgreSQL-ready database architecture.
- Lead qualification.
- Business Context Engine.
- AI-generated outreach messages.
- Conversion prediction.
- Email support.
- WhatsApp message generation.
- LinkedIn message generation.

## Optional

- WhatsApp Business API.
- LinkedIn API.
- Gemini provider.
- Ollama provider.
- Paid email or messaging providers.

## Excluded From Phase 1

- Business logic.
- AI logic.
- CRM features.
- Frontend pages.
- APIs.
- Database models.
- Authentication.
- External integrations.
